How Ramnek Met Salve

    Ramnek and Salve, an odd pair indeed,
Never met before, yet as close as can be.
Ramnek, in a frenzy, grabbed his phone so fast,
Texted his girlfriend, his excitement unsurpassed.

"I can't wait to see you, my dear," he did say,
"So thrilled, I might just show up a day early, by the way!"

    
  Salve sat in her room, phone in her hand,
Anxious and tense, she couldn't stand.
What if someone saw them when they meet?
Her nerves were shot, she couldn't take the heat.
But then she shrugged it, and shook her head,
Realizing her worries were all in her head.
She took a deep breath, and let out a sigh,
    
    Ramnek, oh Ramnek, what a fix he was in,
To leave the house, oh, what a sin!
Raining, dark, and locked out tight,

"I must go out," he said with a pout,
"but  it's locked out outside, and that's no doubt."
thought Ramnek in his head lout, and they played their part,
In this comical, fortunate start.
    
Ramnek, sly as a fox, left the house with glee,
Past the gate, oh, what a sight to see!
His parents' mood, he dared to defy,
For his love, oh my, oh my!
Eager and excited, his heart all aglow,
To meet his love, come rain or snow.
Sneaking away, his happiness uncurbed,
In love's adventure, he was fully absorbed.
    
    Ramnek walked the roads, empty and bare,
Dark, rainy, and cold, but he didn't care.
His love for Salve, it kept him strong,
The streets were barren, everything was close,
But Ramnek's heart, it was all aglow.
For he was eager and excited to meet his love,
And nothing could stop him, not even the rain above.

In the dark and rain, his actions took their toll.
But Ramnek, undeterred, with a smile on his face,
Marched on happily, at his own pace.

In this world where Covid reigns,

Signs warn of jail, if we stray,

Everything looked deserted, empty and the dogs bold
    
    Ramnek, the ninja, dodged the cops,

His love's sweet call, like a siren's song,
Pulled him along, righting every wrong.

Ramnek sprinted, setting his pace.
They huffed and puffed, but he was too fleet,
A slippery rascal, nimble on his feet.
Through alleys and streets, he led them astray,

Ramnek got all wet in the rain,

Sunpreet, so pretty, with a worried frown,

What if Ramnek didn't come, to see his girl?

Ramnek's arrival, oh, so near!

Ramenk's heart, full of love, no fear.
